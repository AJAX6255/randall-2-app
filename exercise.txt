===============================================================================
                       SEMANTIC MACRO OS - EXERCISE TERMINAL PROMPTS
===============================================================================
This file contains curated conversational prompts designed to exercise every
deterministic transform in `analytics.py` and every visual indicator option
supported in `visuals.py` / `orchestrator.py`. 

Copy and paste these prompts directly into the conversational input box at the 
bottom of the dashboard to verify execution.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 1: TESTING CORE ANALYTICAL PRIMITIVES (TRANSFORMS)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Raw Price / Value comparison ("none")
   Prompt: "Plot the raw price history of SPY and GLD side-by-side"

2. Rolling Z-Score ("zscore")
   Prompt: "Show the 60-day rolling Z-score of Bitcoin (BTC)"

3. Rolling Volatility ("volatility")
   Prompt: "Calculate the 30-day rolling annualized volatility of DRAM"

4. Drawdown from Peak ("drawdown")
   Prompt: "Show the percentage drawdown of Bitcoin from its running peak"

5. Rolling Correlation ("correlation")
   Prompt: "Compare SPY and TLT rolling 60-day correlation"

6. Rolling Beta ("beta")
   Prompt: "Calculate the 90-day rolling beta of DRAM relative to SPY"

7. Relative Strength / Ratio ("relative_strength")
   Prompt: "Plot the relative strength ratio of JNK divided by TLT"

8. Rolling PCA Systemic Risk Factor ("pca")
   Prompt: "Calculate the rolling PCA first principal component across SPY, VIX, TLT, and JNK using a 60 day window"

9. Rolling Sharpe Ratio ("sharpe")
   Prompt: "Show the rolling 60-day Sharpe ratio of SPY using FFR as the risk-free rate"

10. Rolling Sortino Ratio ("sortino")
    Prompt: "Show the rolling 60-day Sortino ratio of BTC using FFR as the risk-free rate"

11. Cointegration Spread ("cointegration")
    Prompt: "Plot the rolling cointegration spread of SPY relative to TLT"

12. Drawdown Recovery Duration ("drawdown_duration")
    Prompt: "Calculate the rolling drawdown recovery duration of Bitcoin in business days"

13. Granger Causality p-value ("granger")
    Prompt: "Run a rolling Granger causality test to see if SOFR leads SPY with a window of 60 days"
