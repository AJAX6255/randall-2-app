Why Gemini Flash Specifically

For your workload:

analytical orchestration
semantic interpretation
JSON generation
chart planning

you do NOT need frontier reasoning models most of the time.

Gemini Flash models are:

cheaper
fast
low latency
adequate for orchestration

This matters enormously for conversational dashboards.

Important Architectural Recommendation

The optimal design is probably:

Gemini does:
- intent extraction
- tool selection
- visualization planning

Python engine does:
- all quantitative transforms
- all calculations
- all normalization
- all statistics

Vega-Lite does:
- rendering
- layering
- interaction

That separation is professionally very important.

Most Affordable “Near Future” Architecture

Honestly I think this is probably your strongest near-term stack:

Gemini Flash
    ↓
Structured JSON tool calls
    ↓
Polars transformation engine
    ↓
Altair/Vega-Lite
    ↓
Streamlit UI

because it gives:

openness
affordability
reproducibility
extensibility
semantic interaction

without needing Bloomberg-scale infrastructure.
